定位巴士广告

发表于 2012-02-11 | 更新于 2018-07-24 | 分类于 车辆与交通系统创意

互联网上的定位广告能够根据用户的习惯和网页的内容来投放更加精准的广告。但是在现实世界中，又该如何投放定位广告呢？日前，美国纽约的公交公司就准备测试一种全新的数字广告系统。他们会在公交车的一侧放置一个平板电视用来播放广告，而内置的GPS则可以探测到公交车的位置，从而根据不同的地理位置来播放更具针对性的广告。譬如，途径你家门口的时候，公交车上的广告就会显示附近杂货铺的打折信息等。

不用电的闹钟

发表于 2012-02-11 | 更新于 2018-07-24 | 分类于 环保&公益创意

想要不用电就让闹钟运行，最常用的办法是老式的机械表或者利用太阳能。不过，我们今天为大家介绍的这个设计则采用了更为有趣的设计思路。

它其实是利用电磁感应来充电的，使用者需要来回摇晃闹钟才能为其充电。但是，如果忘记摇晃了，闹钟岂不是无法正常运转了？别担心，它还设计有另外一个独特的功能：每天早晨，预设的时间一到，闹铃响起后，想要关闭闹铃，使用者就必须要不停的摇晃它，直至将电力充满至足以再运转一天为止。既达到起到了闹醒的作用，又非常环保。

涂层显示器

发表于 2012-02-11 | 更新于 2018-07-24 | 分类于 环保&公益创意

我们曾经为大家介绍过几款超薄显示器，其中最薄的甚至能够达到0.3毫米。那么未来的显示器究竟能够做到多么薄呢？

日前，日本的三菱化工和住友化工正在联合开发一种涂层显示器。它的原理类似于我们去年为大家介绍的“太阳能涂层”，将显示所需的OLED与太阳能电池制作成类似于油漆的涂料，然后将这种涂料直接刷在任意的表面上即可获得一个OLED显示器。不仅如此，这种显示器还将直接通过太阳能取电，这样不仅更加环保，而且完全不需要考虑插座的位置，可以随心所欲的放置电视。

现代自行车头盔

发表于 2012-02-11 | 更新于 2018-07-24 | 分类于 车辆与交通系统创意

safety first.
we all know that bicycle helmets commonly feature a hard plastic shell with an elongated shape
that protects your forehead during forward fall, decreasing the force of impact.
this typical form of protective head gear usually features long vents cut through the top of the shell
that provide increased airflow during long distance treks. the ventilation system also helps to absorb your perspiration,
but for the casual biker style and comfort remains tops. here’s a look at some modern takes on the bicycle helmet:


yakkay ‘smart one’ collection
clockwise from top left: paris blue red; milano blue denim; tokyo new jazz; luzern black

danish company yakkay produces bicycle helmets are a take on modern hat classics.
combining safety with appearance, the helmets allow wearers to adapt them according to their personal style.
the shell of the helmet remains the same, ‘hat’ covers’ are available to switch things up.
if you want a new look, don’t change your helmet, just change the cover.


cork bicycle helmet by kévin goupil
images via selectism

kévin goupil’s cork bicycle helmet is fashioned off old french army helmets and is outfitted with
a cushioned suspension wrapped with a sustainable harvested cork hell. the perfect defense mechanism
within the realm of city cycling.


city of new york ‘NYC helmet’ by yves behar / fuse project

a commission by the city of new york, the NYC helmet by san francisco-based yves behar / fuseproject
is composed of a modular system made-up of two components: a protective polystyrene inner shell
and a soft fabric outer cover with integrated straps, giving owners the ability to customize and personalize
the outer cover which separates from the protective shell for easy cleaning.


‘helmet B’ by julien gergignat

the armadillo is an animal recognized for its plates that form a protective shell when it feels threatened.
this was the starting point for the concept of ‘helmet B’ by french designer julien bergignat with patrice mouille.
the helmet body is obtained by cutting a plate of polypropylene protection elements added by molded of EPP
(expanded polypropylene). just fold the wings of the helmet inward and fix them with a pressure clip
and the helmet is ready for use. the selected material has a high absorption power and is durable.


‘helmet B’ in its flat form


fastening the helmet into place using a pressure clip

Helmet B from Opton on Vimeo.


‘viva bike helmet’ by whomadeID + VIVA

whomadeID + VIVA’s challenge when designing the ‘viva bike helmet’ is that they wanted to develop a bike helmet that
looks good as well as provides maximum protection. they came up with a protective head piece that combines finely stitched
genuine leather with a fiberglass shell for better impact-absorption.


‘viva helmet’ in black leather


‘topup: head protection for ‘doyouvélo?’’ by adrien guérin

the concept behind adrien guérin‘s ‘topup head protection’ is not a bicycle helmet per se.
it should be considered more a helmet for daily protection. the design reduces the task of having to carry around
a cumbersome helmet, while bring the user protection through means of comfort. different from the rigid helmet,
‘topup’ utilizes a flexible textile material that is arranged in a honeycomb structure to provide a solution that reconciles
safety and comfort.


‘topup’ has honeycomb structure which can be collapsed and expanded much like an accordian
